ABOUT

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering and am a registered Environmental Engineer with the Saudi Council of Engineering, bringing over 8 years of experience in safety and environmental management. Currently, I serve as the HSE Lead at Almabani General Contractor, where I oversee the Sports Boulevard Foundation Package 6ABC mega project in Riyadh. My expertise includes developing and implementing HSE Management Systems that ensure compliance and foster a culture of safety and environmental sustainability. Throughout my career, I have earned several international certifications, including ISO 14001:2015 EMS Lead Auditor, NEBOSH IGC, IOSH Managing Safely, and Incident Investigation Team Leader. In addition to my professional work, I am committed to social work, which has further enhanced my leadership and teamwork skills.
